TEDxChicago

Lead Performance Curator for one of the most prestigious independently organized TED events in the country.

Client: TEDxChicago
Services: Lead Performance Curation, Event Production, Speaker Coordination, Promotional Strategy
Scope: Chicago, IL

THE STORY

TEDxChicago, one of the most prestigious independently organized TED events in the country, needed leadership in curation and production to deliver world-class experiences to Chicago's innovation community.

THE SOLUTION

As Lead Performance Curator, managed the complete production pipeline for two major events: identifying and securing speakers, coordinating logistics across venues and production teams, developing promotional strategies, and ensuring every element of the attendee experience reflected the caliber that TEDx demands.

IMPACT

1,500+ Attendees
2 Major Events Produced
Lead Performance Curator Role
Speaker Pipeline Built
