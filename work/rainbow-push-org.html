Rainbow PUSH Organizational Support

Comprehensive production support for Rainbow PUSH Coalition's Annual International Convention and entertainment projects that extended the Coalition's cultural reach.

Client: Rainbow PUSH Coalition
Services: International Conference Production, Entertainment Project Management, Event Strategy
Scope: National / International

THE STORY

Rainbow PUSH Coalition's Annual International Convention is one of the most significant gatherings of civil rights leaders, politicians, business executives, and community organizers in the country. The organization needed comprehensive production support for both the International Convention and entertainment-focused projects that extended the Coalition's cultural reach.

THE SOLUTION

Provided strategic organizational support across multiple Rainbow PUSH initiatives, including production management for the Annual International Convention and entertainment projects. Managed logistics, speaker coordination, entertainment programming, and sponsor activation, delivering high-impact events that reinforced the Coalition's position as a premier civil rights organization.
