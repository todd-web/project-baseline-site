Nielsen + Black Girl Magic

A cross-platform national digital campaign elevating Nielsen's brand among African-American consumers.

Client: Nielsen
Services: Creative Campaign, Digital Marketing, Media Strategy
Scope: National (New York, Atlanta, Chicago)

THE STORY

Nielsen, one of the world's premiere media and consumer research entities, needed support to increase their digital footprint among African-American consumers and position Nielsen as the world's leading marketing, consumer information, and media measurement company. To do this, we helped develop concepts and creative campaign assets that increased online exposure of the 2017 DIS AA Consumer Report focusing on Black women.

THE SOLUTION

We developed concepts and creative campaign assets for a cross-platform, media-rich national digital campaign titled "Makings of Her Magic." The campaign was featured nationally, with primary focus on three major markets: New York, Atlanta, and Chicago.

KEY OBJECTIVES

  • Build upon growing digital footprint for Nielsen brand by delivering increased display unit impressions
  • Elevate Nielsen awareness and familiarity among core audience in focus markets
  • Deliver a cross-platform, media rich campaign that is on budget

IMPACT

$350K Media Spend
3 Major Markets
National Digital Campaign
Cross-Platform Media Rich
