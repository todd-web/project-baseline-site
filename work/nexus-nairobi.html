Nexus Nairobi 2023

An international gathering of scientists, engineers, artists, and entrepreneurs from 50+ countries, exploring how space-age thinking can transform life on Earth.

Client: The Jemison Group / 100 Year Starship Foundation
Services: International Event Production, Partnership Development, Strategic Planning
Scope: Nairobi, Kenya (Global, 50+ countries)

THE STORY

Nexus Nairobi was an international gathering bringing together scientists, engineers, artists, educators, and entrepreneurs from 50+ countries to explore how space-age technologies and thinking can transform life on Earth. The event was organized as part of Dr. Mae Jemison's 100 Year Starship initiative.

THE SOLUTION

Led the strategic planning and production support for Nexus Nairobi 2023, coordinating logistics, partnerships, and participant engagement across 50+ countries. The event featured 100+ collaborative projects and reached a global audience of 1M+.
