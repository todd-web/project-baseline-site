Rainbow PUSH + Chevrolet "Money Matters"

A financial literacy and empowerment initiative connecting Chevrolet's brand investment with Rainbow PUSH's authentic community reach.

Client: Rainbow PUSH Coalition / Chevrolet
Services: Financial Literacy Campaign, Brand Partnership, Community Engagement
Scope: National

THE STORY

Rainbow PUSH Coalition partnered with Chevrolet to create "Money Matters," a financial literacy and empowerment initiative targeting underserved communities. The program needed a strategic partner who could bridge corporate objectives with authentic community engagement.

THE SOLUTION

Led the campaign strategy connecting Chevrolet's brand investment with Rainbow PUSH's community reach. Developed the programming framework that educated 1,000+ participants on financial literacy while building authentic brand affinity for Chevrolet within the community.
