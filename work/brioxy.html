Brioxy White House Summit

Convening the country's top millennial innovators of color at the White House to invest in the next generation of leaders.

Client: Brioxy
Services: Event Strategy, Leadership Development, Convening
Scope: National (Washington, D.C.)

THE STORY

The inaugural Brioxy convened the country's top millennial innovators of color to build an infrastructure to invest in young people of color. Brioxy gathered leaders from various sectors including social change/nonprofits, media, government, philanthropy, arts, science, technology and provided a sophisticated balance of personal development, leadership skill building, and access to capital/resources.

THE SOLUTION

The summit included two days in a local venue plus one day inside the White House with senior White House officials, creating an unprecedented platform for emerging diverse leaders to connect with decision-makers and resources at the highest levels.

IMPACT

150+ Innovators Convened
3 Days Summit Duration
