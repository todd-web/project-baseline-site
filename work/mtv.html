MTV + Rainbow PUSH Coalition

A digital activation honoring the 50th anniversary of the March on Washington through the #WhatsYourDream initiative.

Client: MTV / Rainbow PUSH Coalition
Services: Digital Campaign, Social Enterprise, Initiative Development
Scope: National

THE STORY

MTV, Rev. Jesse Jackson Sr., and the Rainbow PUSH Coalition were looking for a way to engage millennials with a digital activation and call-to-action that paid homage to Dr. Martin Luther King Jr.'s March on Washington. We created a solution that resulted in a collaboration with MTV's social enterprise arm MTV ACT to launch the #WhatsYourDream initiative.

THE SOLUTION

The initiative successfully launched Wednesday August 28th, 2013, on the official 50th anniversary of the march, through two primary engagements: an open letter from Reverend Jesse L. Jackson Sr. and a social media/photo campaign around the #WhatsYourDream hashtag. The ultimate call to action was to have participants pledge to do their part to keep the dream alive by visiting YourDreamYourPledge.com.
