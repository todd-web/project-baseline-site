South African Strategic Engagement

Building lasting projects and relationships between the US and South Africa through cultural exchange and community engagement.

Client: Multiple Partners
Services: Market Entry Strategy, Cultural Exchange, Community Engagement
Scope: US + South Africa

THE STORY

Just one year after the tragic death of South Africa's iconic leader Nelson Mandela, the Office of the Consul General was planning the annual memorial celebration in Chicago. The city's long-lasting connection with the country of South Africa meant there were already deep relationships built within specific communities. Through involvement with this initiative, a spark was ignited and from there continued development of projects and relationships between the US and South Africa.

THE SOLUTION

We developed a multi-faceted strategic engagement between the US and South Africa, encompassing cultural exchange, brand development, and community engagement initiatives.

KEY PROJECTS

  • IGNITE Global Connection Series
  • Alodia Natural Hair Care Pop-up Experience
  • From The Roots Cultural Impact Exchange Program
  • South African Consulate General of Chicago Community Engagement Initiatives
  • Brioxy International Community Development Project
