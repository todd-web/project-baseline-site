Donda's House / Art of Culture

Board-level leadership, communications strategy, and fundraising for an arts education nonprofit founded in honor of Dr. Donda West.

Client: Donda's House (Art of Culture)
Services: Board Leadership, Communications Strategy, Fundraising, Youth Engagement
Scope: Chicago, IL

THE STORY

Donda's House, the arts education nonprofit founded in honor of Kanye West's mother Dr. Donda West, needed board-level leadership to scale its impact, build sustainable funding, and strengthen its communications strategy.

THE SOLUTION

As a member of the Board of Directors, helped shape the organization's strategic direction and public communications. Partnered with Kanye West, Rhymefest, and other notable figures to expand the organization's reach and deepen its impact on Chicago's youth arts community.
