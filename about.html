ABOUT PROJECT BASELINE

Strategic consulting built on innovation, cultural intelligence, and two decades of results.

FULL-CIRCLE SOLUTIONS FROM ONE SOURCE

Project Baseline was founded in 2007 by Todd Walton, a connector and builder whose work spans culture, business, civic life, technology, and global community. Not because he chases industries, but because he genuinely understands people across every one of those lines.

From global engagement initiatives with Dr. Mae Jemison to launching Complex Community Week in Chicago, from Chevrolet partnerships with Rainbow PUSH to advising on South African market entry, our work is built on the kind of relationships that only come from genuine belonging in every room we enter.

We bring cultural fluency, strategic discipline, and the patience to stay long enough for results to compound. That combination has generated over 25 billion impressions across 20+ campaigns and built partnerships that span decades, not quarters.

HOW WE WORK

A demonstrated collaborative methodology sharpened over time to deliver results.

01

ASSESSMENT

We start with a deep-dive into your current state, goals, and challenges. Understanding your organization, your audience, and the landscape you're operating in. No templates. No assumptions. Real listening that builds real understanding.

02

STRATEGY

Building the roadmap with clear objectives and measurable outcomes. We connect the dots between your brand identity, your audience, and your business objectives to create a plan that's both ambitious and executable.

03

DESIGN

Creative solutions tailored to your brand identity and audience. Whether it's campaign creative, experiential concepts, or digital strategy, every element is designed with intention and cultural intelligence.

04

Bringing strategies to life with disciplined execution. This is where plans become real: assets are built, partnerships are activated, teams are aligned, and timelines are managed with precision.

05

Deploying solutions with attention to detail and timeline management. Launch day is just the beginning. We manage every moving piece to ensure the work lands the way it was designed to.

Ongoing oversight to ensure sustained impact and optimization. We stay. We measure. We adjust. Because the real value of any engagement is what compounds over time, not what launches with fanfare.
